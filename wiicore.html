WiiCore.js

The Open-Source Implementation of Wii Shop Channel's API.
Easy to implement. WiiCore is as easy as bundling into your project and referencing the JS API that you wish to use in your head.
Open-Source. WiiCore.js is licensed under AGPLv3. Feel free to contribute or use in your projects.
Functional. It's one of the only working Wii Shop APIs as of 2019.
WiiCore.js does not work on the Internet Channel on Wii, PC Browsers like Google Chrome or any other browser that is not the Wii Shop Channel.

     WiiCore.js is part of The Open Shop Channel Project.
